WHO WE ARE?

An Egyptian company established in 2019 in Cairo, Egypt. Geomakani is a Pioneering Business Development System Integrator and Geospatial Events Company committed to driving innovation and empowering organizations. We specialize in integrating cutting-edge geospatial technologies to optimize business processes, enhance efficiency, and unlock new growth opportunities in the Middle East & Africa. Geomakani is taking the lead of working in the Middle East & Africa under the umbrella of STADIA Group.
